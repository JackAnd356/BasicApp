Recipe of the Week

Herb Crusted Chicken

Step 1 - Combine Seasoning - Mix all the seasonings together in a small bowl.
Step 2 - Flatten Chicken Breast - Place the chicken breasts in between plastic wrap. Use a large wooden spoon or mallet to flatten the chicken breasts.
Step 3 - Season Chicken - Season both sides of the chicken breasts with the seasoning mixture.
Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 10 mins
